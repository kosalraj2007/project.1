About Kamal Haasan

Haasan was born on 7 November 1954, in a Tamil Iyengar Brahmin family, to D. Srinivasan, who was a lawyer and freedom fighter, and Rajalakshmi, who was a housewife. Haasan was initially named as Parthasarathy. His father later changed his name to Kamal Haasan.Here are some facts about Kamal Haasan: Birth: Born Parthasarathy Srinivasan on November 7, 1954 in Paramakudi, Tamil Nadu Career: A multi-faceted actor, director, producer, screenwriter, choreographer, singer, lyricist, and television presenter Known for: His work in Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Hindi, and Bengali films Debut: As a child actor in the 1960 film Kalathoor Kannamma Awards: Won four National Film Awards, nine Tamil Nadu State Film Awards, four Nandi Awards, one Rashtrapati Award, two Filmfare Awards, and the Padma Shri in 1990 Other honors: The Padma Bhushan in 2014 and the Order of Arts and Letters (Chevalier) in 2016 Known for his roles: In Hey Ram (2000), Haasan played Saket Ram, a man who seeks revenge after a personal tragedy Known for his makeup: Haasan trained with Oscar-winning makeup artist Michael Westmore and used his skills in films like Dasavaratharam and Thoongaa Nagaram Political party: Has been a member of the Makkaldhi Maiam since 2018 Family: Has two daughters, Shruti Haasan and Akshara Haasan, from his second marriage
